Cricket Score

The project includes simulating a cricket match between two teams. The first team bats first and accumulates runs until all their wickets fall. After the innings of the first team, the second team will try to chase the target set by team 1. If team 2 wins the required race, a message is displayed indicating that team 2 wins. This program aims to provide a clear and interesting picture of the cricket tournament scenario, so that users can enjoy the game and understand the dynamics of chasing scores in cricket

Project Link : https://milan6002.github.io/CricketScore/
